Controls
  • Wall: click-drag to draw static walls
  • Box / Circle / Polygon: click to place objects
  • Launcher: click-drag from point to set direction, release to fire
  • Gravity Mass: click to place a gravity source (yellow)
  • Eraser: click to remove objects under cursor
  • Clear: remove all non-boundary objects
  • Adjust global gravity and mass strength with sliders.
